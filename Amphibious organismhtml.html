Amphibious organism

Python:A large reptile of the family Pythonidae. The common body length is 3-5 meters, with a dark brown spear-shaped spot on the back of the head and neck, a black longitudinal spot on the side of the head, yellowish-white on the abdomen of the head, tan, taupe or yellow on the back of the body, and large black border clouded leopard-like markings on the back and sides.

Crocodiles:It reptiles belonging to the family Crocodylidae and Crocodylidae, There are 23 species in 8 genera. Crocodiles have long torsos; Muscular; The head is flat, the skin of the head is close to the skull, and the skull is firmly connected and cannot be moved; The teeth are conical and grow in the alveolar, with more than 25 teeth on each side; The tongue is short and flat and cannot be extended; The eyes are small and protruding; The tail is stout and slightly flat; Stubby limbs; The outer nostril is located on the back of the rostral end and has valves on it; It has scales that look like armor.
