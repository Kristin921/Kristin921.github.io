Great white shark:It is the largest predatory fish in the world, the main natural enemy of many Marine mammals, but also will hunt seabirds and other fish and other Marine life can be caught. Like most sharks, great whites are opportunists, attacking the right prey at the right time and ignoring undesirable prey. In contrast, there are far more recorded bull shark attacks on humans than great white shark attacks, and some bull shark attacks are often mistaken for great white shark attacks.
